about me

My name is Milan Dobrota. I started using Ruby on Rails back in 2008 and never looked back. Designed for developers productivity and happiness, Ruby and Rails transformed the way software development is done. Over the years, the framework evolved into an enterprise ready solution, and was adopted by companies of all sizes. This was no accident - Ruby community is nothing less than amazing and I'm glad to be a part of it.

A few years ago I decided to give back and as a result I contributed to the Rails framework. I also worked on a variety of projects for companies ranging from small startups to media giants such as Chicago Tribune and Los Angeles Times. I was involved in all stages of software development cycle, from idea conception, requirements gathering, architecting and designing a solution to leading teams and implementing features myself. I also mentored developers who were not familiar with the language or framework.

I believe that writing code is art. Developers don't write code (just) for computers anymore. Great code follows conventions, makes sense, and is beautiful. Great code is the product of developer creativity and inspiration.
